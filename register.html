Mahatennis Foundation is a trust which has been working for the cause of promotion and dissemination of tennis since last 6 years. Mahatennis Foundation is running a Tennis Academy at Balewadi Stadium. Mahatennis Foundation provides sponsorship to ranked players in Maharashtra and India. Also, Mahatennis Foundation is currently working with renowned academy Juan Carlos ferrero Academy in Spain to promote tennis in India at a higher level.
